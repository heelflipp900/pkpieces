Overwatch Pachimari Mini Amigurumi

Materials

  • Size 4 Worsted weight yarn in white, light yan and light green
    • Impeccable loops & threads in "White"
    • Impeccable loops & threads in "Heather"
    • Big twist value in "Lime"
  • F hook (3.75mm)
  • B hook (2.25 mm)
  • Pink felt
  • Black embroidery thread
  • Black brads from michaels (for the eyes)

Majority of this pattern uses: ½ Worsted Weight (½ WW) = 2 ply yarn
(I do not have size 2 yarn on hand, so I split size 4 yarn: 4 ply into 2 ply)

Body (F hook)

R1: 6 sc in a magic ring using tan
R2: (sc, inc) around [9]
R3: (sc2, inc) around [12]
R4-5: sc around [12]
R6: (sc2, dec) around [9]
R7: (sc, dec) around [6]
R8: sc around [6]
Ultimate finish closed to make the pointy tip. (planet june)

Face: ½ WW B Hook

R1: 6 sc in a magic ring using white
R2: inc around [12]
R3: (sc, inc) around [18]
R4: sc around using tan [18]
⇒ Insert 2 mini black brads for the eyes
⇒ Use 2 ply of black embroidery thread and embroider any kind of smile you want.

Legs: ½ WW C Hook *** 4 separate parts (pt)

R1: 3 sc in a magic ring
R2: inc around [6]
R3: sc around [6]
R4: sc 2, inc 2, sc2 [8]
R5: sc around [8]
R6: ch1, hdc 4 [4]
R7: ch1, sc 4 [4]
⇒ pt #1: R1-6
⇒ pt #2: R1-7
⇒ connect pc #1 and #2 with whip stitch
⇒ pt #3 and #4: R1-5
Pin together as shown in picture, whip stitch and connect to body. Stuff each leg lightly.

Assembly

  • Pin face to body then whip stitch onto body through BLO.
  • Cut 2 small pcs of pink felt and use glue gun to attach.
