Crochet pattern by Ninian, my sister :)

Materials

  • Caron Simply Soft Yarn - Worsted weight 4 Acrylic
  • 4.00 mm size hook
  • Yarn needle
BPDC - back post double crochet
FPDC - front post double crochet
https://youtu.be/S42_486HrEU
  1. 10 hdc in a magic circle [10]
  2. 2 hdc in each st, sl st to join [20]
  3. [1 hdc, inc] around, sl st to join [30]
  4. [2 hdc, inc] around, sl st to join [40]
  5. [3 hdc, inc] around, sl st to join [50]
  6. Ch 2 (not a st), [dc] around [50]
  7. Ch 1 (not a st), [bpdc, fpdc] until end, sl st to end [50]
  8. Repeat R7 until desired height is achieved, fo and weave in ends
